# Annual Card Fee Waiver Policy (Demo)

## Scope

Only **annual card fee** waivers are in scope. Other fee types (cash advance, late payment, FX markup) are **not** waived through this demo path.

## Eligibility

The **Radish Cashback Card** and equivalent retail cards in the demo dataset are eligible when the card status is **active**.

## Frequency

At most **one approved annual card fee waiver per rolling 12 months** per card fee category. Repeat requests inside that window are **rejected** with a clear reason.

## Assessment

Decisions in this demo are **deterministic** from request history—no manual underwriting queue.

## Out of scope

Waivers for corporate cards, private banking cards, or non-annual fee types are **unsupported** in this demo.
