# Branch Services Guide

## Full branch vs auto-lobby

- **Full branch** — staffed counters, consultations, and complex servicing during published hours.
- **Auto-lobby** — **self-service devices only** (ATMs, intelligent deposit machines). No staffed teller line.

## Services

Full branches support account servicing, card replacement requests (where product rules allow), and general inquiries. Auto-lobbies support **self-service banking only**—use a full branch for assisted paperwork.
