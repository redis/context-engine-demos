# Radish Bank Fixed Deposit FAQ

## Tenures and rates

Radish Bank offers **6-month** and **12-month** Singapore dollar fixed deposits. Published rates match the active **FixedDepositPlan** records (for example **FD6** and **FD12**).

## Minimum deposit

The minimum placement is **SGD 1,000** per plan unless a promotion states otherwise.

## Interest

Interest accrues for the locked tenure. At maturity, principal and interest are credited according to product rules in force on the booking date.

## Early withdrawal and early termination

**Early termination** (closing or **terminating** the fixed deposit before the maturity date) is the same as **early withdrawal** in this product. Withdrawing or **terminating** early may **reduce or forfeit** interest. In practice this is the **penalty** for breaking the fixed deposit early: you may lose part or all of the interest you would have earned to maturity. **Early termination penalties** are therefore interest reductions or forfeiture, not a separate cash fee in this demo. The bank applies a simple adjustment for demo purposes; always confirm the exact **penalties** or adjustments before **terminating** or breaking a deposit.

## Eligibility

Placements are available from **eligible active** SGD accounts shown in the customer profile. The demo supports amounts **below SGD 10,000** per placement request.

## How to book

Use the in-app **place fixed deposit** flow with a chosen plan, amount, and funding account. Staff-assisted bookings in this demo follow the same validation rules.
