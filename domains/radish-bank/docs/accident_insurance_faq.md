# Radish Bank Accident Insurance FAQ

## Plans

Two retail accident products are offered:

- **Basic Accident Cover** — lower annual premium, moderate coverage ceiling.
- **Plus Accident Cover** — higher annual premium, higher coverage ceiling.

Premium and coverage figures match **InsurancePlan** rows **INS_BASIC** and **INS_PLUS**.

## Waiting period

A short **7-day** waiting period applies before certain benefits activate. This is a simplified demo rule, not a legal contract.

## Exclusions

High-risk sports, self-inflicted injury, and fraud are excluded in plain language. This FAQ is **not** the full policy wording.

## Purchase and cancellation

Customers may purchase one unit of each plan for the demo portfolio. Cancellation mid-term follows the tariff grid on the policy schedule (simplified in this demo).
